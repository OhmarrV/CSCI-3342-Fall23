Top 5 Whataburger Burgers Ranked

An Omar Velazquez "Food Review" List

  • 5.

    • Bacon & Cheese

    Review: The Bacon cheese burger from whataburger is a go to staple at whataburger for many people. The only downside is that it lacks that "spice" that some of the limited time or specialty burgers have. Other than maybe being a little boring, the taste is FAR from that and makes a perfect order.


  • 4.

    • The Whataburger®

    Review: The Orignal, Classic, Foundational burger, the number 1. is the original whataburger burger. There is nothing like it... The number 1. is the OG burger that is the perfect combination of meat, buns, veggies, and sauce of your choice. Like I said, there really is nothing like it.


  • 3.

    • Mushroom Swiss Burger

    Review: OK, HEAR ME OUT. As a prior mushroom hater, I would have never imagined liking this burger. But remeber the day I recieved the wrong order and ended up with a 3 mushroom swiss burgers in my bag instead of my avocado bacon burger... I was truly gifted from the heavens above. I definitely recommend everyone to try this burger, you will NOT be disappointed!


  • 2.

    • Avocado Bacon Burger

    \

    Review: Oh what used to be. The avocado bacon burger used to be my all time favorite. I can't even recal the amount of years I went ordering "The avocado bacon burger, no onions, and on regular buns." (MUST SAY REGULAR BUNS) Unfortunately, as of the last year, the quality of this burger has gone downhill drastically, but I will keep it up top for the personal memories I have with it.


  • Honorable Mention

    • Breakfast Burger

    Review: The only reason this burger is not on this top 5 list is because you CAN'T order it all day!! Only available until 11am. The breakfast burger is quite literally, THE breakfast burger.


  • Number 1.

    • Southern Bacon Double

    Review: There are no words that can describe how much I LOVE this burger. I just recently tried it, as it's a new limited release, and it is perfect. It is a 10/10 and I pray that it becomes a fan favorite addition to the menu. Please...
